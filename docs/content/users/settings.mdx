# Settings

Customize your Auktiva account and preferences.

## Accessing Settings

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **"Settings"**

## Profile Settings

### Display Name

Your name shown to other users.

### Email Address

Your login email.

## Security Settings

### Changing Password

1. Go to Settings
2. Find "Change Password"
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

## Email Notifications

Control which email notifications you receive.

### New Item Notifications

Get notified via email when a new item is added to an auction you're a member of.

- **Enabled** - Receive emails for new items
- **Disabled** (default) - No emails for new items

### Outbid Notifications

Get notified via email when someone outbids you on an item.

- **Enabled** - Receive emails when outbid
- **Disabled** (default) - No outbid emails

<Callout type="info">
  Email notifications are separate from in-app notifications. You'll still see
  notifications in the app even if email is disabled.
</Callout>

## Account Actions

### Logging Out

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **"Sign Out"**

### Deleting Your Account

<Callout type="error">
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This action complies with
  GDPR data deletion requirements.
</Callout>

#### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the bottom to find **"Delete Account"** section
3. Click **"Delete My Account"** button
4. A confirmation modal will appear

#### If You Own Auctions

If you are the owner of any auctions, you must decide what to do with each one before deleting your account:

**Option 1: Transfer Ownership**

- Select "Transfer ownership" for the auction
- Enter the email address of the new owner
- The new owner must be a registered Auktiva user
- They will become the new owner with full control

**Option 2: Delete Auction**

- Select "Delete auction"
- The auction and all its items, bids, and data will be permanently deleted

<Callout type="warning">
  You cannot delete your account while you are the Deployment Admin. You must
  first transfer deployment admin rights to another user in the Deployment
  Administration section.
</Callout>

#### Identity Verification

To confirm deletion, you must verify your identity:

- **Password accounts**: Enter your current password
- **Google OAuth accounts**: Type your email address exactly as shown

#### What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your profile information (name, email, password)
- Your user settings and preferences
- Your auction memberships
- Auctions you own (if you chose to delete them)
- Items you created
- Your bid history
- Your notifications
- Connected OAuth accounts (Google)

<Callout type="info">
  If you transferred auction ownership instead of deleting, those auctions and
  their data will remain for the new owner and other participants.
</Callout>

#### After Deletion

- You will be immediately signed out
- You cannot recover your account
- You can create a new account with the same email if desired

# Settings

Customize your Auktiva account and preferences.

## Accessing Settings

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **"Settings"**

## Profile Settings

### Display Name
Your name shown to other users.

### Email Address
Your login email.

## Security Settings

### Changing Password

1. Go to Settings
2. Find "Change Password"
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

## Email Notifications

Control which email notifications you receive.

### New Item Notifications
Get notified via email when a new item is added to an auction you're a member of.

- **Enabled** - Receive emails for new items
- **Disabled** (default) - No emails for new items

### Outbid Notifications
Get notified via email when someone outbids you on an item.

- **Enabled** - Receive emails when outbid
- **Disabled** (default) - No outbid emails

<Callout type="info">
  Email notifications are separate from in-app notifications. You'll still see notifications in the app even if email is disabled.
</Callout>

## Display Preferences

### Theme

Choose your preferred color scheme:
- **Light** - Bright background
- **Dark** - Dark background
- **System** - Match device settings

## Account Actions

### Logging Out

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **"Sign Out"**

### Deleting Your Account

<Callout type="error">
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Callout>

1. Go to Settings
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click **"Delete Account"**
4. Confirm with your password

**What happens:**
- Account is permanently deleted
- Your bids remain (shown as "Deleted User")
- You cannot recover this account

# Bidding

This guide explains how to place bids and track your bidding activity.

## Placing a Bid

1. Navigate to the item
2. View the current highest bid
3. Enter your bid amount
4. Click **"Place Bid"**

### Bid Requirements

- Must be higher than the current highest bid
- If no bids exist, must meet the starting bid
- Bids are final and cannot be retracted

## Anonymous Bidding

Depending on auction settings:

- **Visible** - Your name appears next to bids
- **Anonymous** - Identity hidden from other bidders
- **Per-Bid Choice** - You choose for each bid

## Tracking Your Bids

### Dashboard

Your dashboard shows items you've bid on:

- Your highest bid
- Current highest bid
- Status: Winning or Outbid

### Bid Status

| Status      | Meaning                       |
| ----------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Winning** | Your bid is currently highest |
| **Outbid**  | Someone bid higher            |
| **Won**     | Item ended, you won           |
| **Lost**    | Item ended, someone else won  |

## Notifications

You'll receive notifications when:

- Someone outbids you
- An auction is ending soon
- You win an item

## Winning an Item

When an item ends and you have the highest bid:

1. You receive a winning notification
2. The item shows "Won" status
3. Contact the auction organizer for next steps

<Callout type="info">

</Callout>

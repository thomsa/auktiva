# Getting Started

This guide will help you create your account and get started with Auktiva.

## Creating an Account

### Option 1: Email & Password

1. Visit the Auktiva homepage
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Register"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Name** - Your display name
   - **Email** - Used for login
   - **Password** - Minimum 6 characters
4. Click **"Create Account"**

### Option 2: Sign in with Google or Microsoft

If your Auktiva instance has OAuth enabled, you can sign up instantly:

1. Click **"Google"** or **"Microsoft"** on the login or register page
2. Select your account
3. You're automatically signed in with your profile

## The Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard showing:

### Your Auctions

All auctions you've created or joined appear as cards showing:

- Auction name and thumbnail
- Your role (Owner, Admin, Creator, or Bidder)
- Number of items and members

### Your Active Bids

Items you've bid on with:

- Current bid amount
- Whether you're winning or outbid

## Creating Your First Auction

1. Click **"Create Auction"** on your dashboard
2. Fill in the auction details
3. Configure settings
4. Click **"Create Auction"**

## Joining an Auction

### Open Auctions

Click to view and start bidding.

### Invite Link

Click the link to join automatically.

### Email Invitation

Click the link in the email to accept.

# Notifications

Stay informed about auction activity with Auktiva's notification system.

## Notification Types

### Outbid Notifications
When someone places a higher bid than yours.

### Auction Ending Soon
When an auction you're in is about to end.

### Item Won
When an item you bid on ends and you won.

### Item Lost
When an item you bid on ends and someone else won.

## Accessing Notifications

### Notification Bell

The bell icon in the top navigation shows:
- Red badge with unread count
- Click to open dropdown
- Recent notifications

### Notification Dropdown

- Unread notifications are highlighted
- Click a notification to go to the item
- Click "Mark all as read" to clear

## Real-Time Updates

Notifications update automatically:
- New notifications appear without refreshing
- Badge count updates in real-time
- Polling interval: every 30 seconds

- Check notifications regularly during active auctions
- Pay attention to outbid notifications
- Watch for auction ending reminders

# Notifications

Stay informed about auction activity with Auktiva's notification system.

## Notification Types

### New Item Added

When a new item is added to an auction you're a member of. Shows the item name, a preview of the description, and the item's image.

### Outbid Notifications

When someone places a higher bid than yours.

### Auction Ending Soon

When an auction you're in is about to end.

### Item Won

When an item you bid on ends and you won.

### Item Lost

When an item you bid on ends and someone else won.

## Accessing Notifications

### Notification Bell

The bell icon in the top navigation shows:

- Red badge with unread count
- Click to open dropdown
- Recent notifications

### Notification Dropdown

- Unread notifications are highlighted
- Click a notification to go to the item
- Click "Mark all as read" to clear

## Real-Time Updates

Notifications update automatically:

- New notifications appear without refreshing
- Badge count updates in real-time
- Polling interval: every 30 seconds

## Email Notifications

In addition to in-app notifications, Auktiva can send you email notifications for important events.

### Email Types

- **Welcome Email** - Sent when you create your account
- **Auction Invites** - When someone invites you to an auction
- **New Items** - When items are added to auctions you're in
- **Outbid Alerts** - When someone outbids you

### Managing Email Preferences

You can control email notifications in **Settings â†’ Email Notifications**:

1. Go to Settings
2. Find "Email Notifications" section
3. Toggle notifications on/off:
   - **New item notifications** - Emails for new auction items
   - **Outbid notifications** - Emails when you're outbid

<Callout type="info">
  Disabling email notifications doesn't affect in-app notifications. You'll

## Best Practices

- Check notifications regularly during active auctions
- Pay attention to outbid notifications
- Watch for auction ending reminders
- Enable email notifications for auctions you care about
